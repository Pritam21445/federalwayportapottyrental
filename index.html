Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a broker or do you own the toilets?

Both, and that's our strength. We operate our own assets in key markets and have an exclusive, high-performance network everywhere else. You get the benefit of a single, accountable provider with true national reach.

What's your "Triple-Check" sanitation process?

It’s our proprietary system: 1) Mechanical deep clean and pump-out. 2) Chemical sanitation and high-pressure rinse. 3) A final pre-delivery inspection and stocking. We take cleanliness seriously.

Why is your price sometimes different from cheap online quotes?

Because we price for reliability. Our quote includes a clean, quality unit, on-time delivery, and no surprise fees. Lower prices often lead to older units, late deliveries, or surprise fuel surcharges.

What happens if there is an issue on-site?

You have a direct line to your account manager. No call centers. We solve problems fast.

Do you offer long-term rental contracts?

Yes, we specialize in long-term contracts for construction, industrial, and government clients with preferred pricing and dedicated service schedules.
